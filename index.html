Fuck the Rocket!

At one point you've been stranded at a TTC stop wondering what's best, the wait or the walk.

This app compares the time you'll spend walking to your destination against the wait for the next TTC 'rocket' to your location.

Enter your location and destination and find out whether you should:

Ride the rocket!

This project was made by @ Enzofromspace

All results are approximations and do not take into account: Traffic, weather, or personal walking speed.
